VISUS AI is an intelligent maritime navigation platform that enhances situational awareness and decision-making through advanced AI models — on your boat, PC or iPad, with any camera you can attach to the vessel.

Advanced route and decision assistance powered by maritime navigation models trained for real conditions.

Computer Vision (Pro)

YOLO models detect obstacles, vessels, buoys and environmental conditions in real time from your boat camera.

Situational Awareness

Maps, AIS and environmental models combined for safer, smarter decisions at sea.

Any Camera, Any Screen
